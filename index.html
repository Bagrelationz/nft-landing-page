The Art Hall

The Art Hall is all about NFT's. We will create and launch the first ever community owned nft project. Art Hall Nft holders will earn rewards from the launch, as well as, secondary sales of the nft project that they will help shape and design. The Art Hall will also be launching a nft marketplace that will co-owned by the community. The Art Hall aims to be the premier place where anyone can share their ideas and bring them to nft digital reality. The Art Hall will continue to launch community created and owned nft project multiple times per year. Holders of the limited supply of 3000 nfts will earn passive income forever. The Art Hall will also be home to Social and Smooth, a premier media agency, that will provide numerous nft services and education to our community, as well as the public. There will only be 3000 Art Hall nfts, of which, 200 will be rare and have extra benefits and utility. We have at least 2 community projects planned for 2022 and special project giveaways for our rare nft holders. The first community nft project will begin from day 1 after mint.
